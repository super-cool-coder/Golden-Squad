Not going to lie, Hifz is pretty stressful. But remember, it is an ultimately special and rewarding business. After all, it is memorisation of the Holy Quran.

To perform Hifz properly, 3 things have to be delivered everyday:

  • Sabaq (Your new memorisation every day)
  • Previous Sabaq (The 7 pages preceding your Sabaq)
  • Daur (Revision of the other Juz [volumes] memorised - usually 1/4 or 1/2 of a Juz at a time)

You get so many rewards from reading Quran - you get 10 rewards for every letter read and an it is an intercessor its reciter! When memorised, it gives you an excelling status in Jannah (Paradise)!

Pre-Hifz is the memorisation in the first year - usually of the first 3-5 Juz. There are 10 main steps to this:

  1. Makhaarij Theory & Practical - 2 weeks
  2. Qaidah crash course (BTQ 1) - 6 weeks
  3. Tajweed theory and practical (BTQ 2) - 2 weeks
  4. Tajweed implementation on Juz Amma + fluency rules - 4 weeks
  5. Juz Amma group Mashq - continue from beginning
  6. Tanzil.net Quran Navigator - continue from beginning
  7. Hifz Nazirah Skills (Nazirah Alaa Tarteebil Hifz) - 4 weeks
  8. Memorise Juz Amma and Continue Hifz Nazirah skills - 6 weeks
  9. Complete HNS and memorisation of next 2 Juz - 12 weeks
  10. Advance Hifz Nazirah skills on next 7 Juz - 4 weeks
